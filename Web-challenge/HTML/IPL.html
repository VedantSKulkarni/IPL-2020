IPL (Indian Premier League)



The IPL or the Indian Premier League is a cricket series contaning 8 teams. These teams play 14 total matches against each other. These teams are owned by companies or people. Each team has 11 players and a few substitute players. Before the IPL series starts these individuals or comapany representatives come aand have a meeting where they bid for each player and the player with the highest points gets the player. This is called auctions. The player receives that much money as fee.

The teams are:

  • Mumbai Indians (MI)
  • Chennai Super Kings (CSK)
  • Sun Risers Hyderabad (SRH)
  • Kolkata Knight riders (KKR)
  • Rajasthan Royals (RR)
  • Royal Challengers Bangalore (RCB)
  • Delhi Capitals (DC)
  • Kings XI Punjab (KXIP)
